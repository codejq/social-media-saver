Privacy Policy

Social Media Saver Extension

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Social Media Saver browser extension ("we", "our", or "the Extension") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

The Extension may access the following information solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Social media page content visible in your browser tab when you activate the Extension.
  • User preferences and settings you configure within the Extension (e.g., export format, default options).

We do not collect personal identifying information such as your name, email address, or account credentials.

How We Use Your Information

Any information accessed by the Extension is used exclusively to:

  • Save and export social media content as requested by you.
  • Store your preferences locally in your browser for a consistent experience.

Data Storage and Security

All data processed by the Extension is stored locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage mechanisms. We do not transmit, upload, or share your data with any external servers or third parties.

  • Saved content is stored in your browser's local storage or as downloaded files on your device.
  • Extension settings are persisted using the browser's extension storage API.
  • No data leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share or export it.

Data Policy

This section outlines our commitments regarding how data is handled, retained, and protected.

  • Data Collection: We follow a minimal data collection approach. The Extension only accesses page content when you explicitly trigger a save action.
  • Data Retention: Saved content remains on your device until you choose to delete it. We do not maintain any copies of your data.
  • Data Sharing: We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your data with any third parties.
  • Data Deletion: You can remove all Extension data at any time by clearing your browser's extension storage or uninstalling the Extension.
  • Third-Party Services: The Extension does not integrate with or send data to any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
  • Children's Data: The Extension does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

Permissions

The Extension may request browser permissions necessary for its operation, such as:

  • Active Tab: To read the content of the current tab when you activate the Extension.
  • Storage: To save your preferences and settings locally.
  • Downloads: To save exported content to your device.

These permissions are used only for their stated purposes and nothing more.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on the project's repository.
