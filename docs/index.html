Features

Everything you need to own your social media content.

💾

Universal Save Button

A non-intrusive "Save" button is injected beside every post, image, and video on supported platforms.

🌐

Multi-Platform Support

Works across Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, Reddit, and Pinterest.

🔒

Privacy First

All data stays on your device. No telemetry, no tracking, no data sent to external servers without your action.

🚀

Flexible Destinations

Publish to WordPress, Drupal, Micropub endpoints, custom APIs, or save locally as HTML files.

🔄

Background Sync

Queue-based background processing with automatic retry, network awareness, and real-time status tracking.

📄

Full Content Extraction

Captures text, images, videos, metadata, hashtags, engagement metrics, and author info from every post.

How It Works

Three steps to take control of your content.

1

Install the Extension

Add Social Media Saver to Chrome, Firefox, or Edge from your browser's extension store.

2

Browse Social Media

Visit any supported platform. A "Save" button appears on every post automatically.

3

Save & Publish

Click save to store content locally or publish it to your configured destination in the background.
